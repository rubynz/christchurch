This month we have Albert Nisbet with a topic dear to my heart - self hosting. Having just rebuilt my personal server I'm keen to hear what I should have done differently ;)  

> Now may be a better time than ever to bring your data back home - but what does it take to actually build and run a self-hosting setup you can count on? In this talk, I'll cover the why, the what and the how of self-hosting. Featuring: cool open-source applications, philosophical considerations, and a few things that have surprised/hurt me on my journey to a system I now rely on every day. Bring your own experiences to add to the discussion and we all might learn a thing or two!
